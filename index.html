I'am Isabella

Since beginning my journey as a Web Developer, Ux designer and digital marketing specialist nearly 3 years ago, I've done remote work for agencies, consulted for startups, and collaborated with talented people to create digital products for both business and consumer use.

Outside of work, I enjoy photography, hiking, and a warm cup coffee.
